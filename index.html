Chapter Two

Ayel, I don't think I've ever quite known how to say this without overthinking every word, so I'm just going to say it simply: knowing you has been one of the easiest and most confusing things that's ever happened to me — easy, because being around you never feels like work, and confusing, because I never expected someone who teases me this much to also be the person I look forward to talking to every single day.

You've become the person I want to tell things to first. The good days, the odd days, the days I don't have anything interesting to say at all — I still want to tell you about them. That's new for me. That's you.

So here's this small corner of the internet, made quietly and a little nervously, just to say: thank you for being my favorite nemesis, and for making all my overthinking feel worth it.

— Jeon
